Subjects: 5 to 8 people who are familiar with smartphone use have volunteered to participate in our experiment. All of them were capable smartphone users, with the ability to touch an app icon proficiently, as well as navigate an app they have not used before.

Equipment: To complete the experiment the team will need the following items.

  • A Smart Phone with the ‘Xiaomi Home Application’ loaded on the device
  • A Xiaomi Smart-Lamp/ Smart Light
  • A Standard Desktop Lamp with a physical on/ off switch
  • Desk
  • Clear space of at least 3 metres that a subject can move around
